Yu WANG

Assistant Researcher

Nanjing University

Biography

I’m an assistant researcher at the Software Engineering Group (SEG) at Nanjing University. My research interests lie in software testing and program language, including static and dynamic analysis. Recently, I focus on improving the performance of program analysis by machine learning.
